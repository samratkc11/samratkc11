Welcome to Our School

Our school is dedicated to nurturing students' intellectual, social, and emotional growth. We provide a dynamic curriculum that empowers each student to reach their highest potential.It is located in kritipur kathmandu.It has many branches all over the Nepal.Most of the students come here for better studies and for better sports involvement.It is fully hostel based school related with Armed Police Force.All over there are above 850 students and more than 30 teachers in this school.The quantity of boys are very much more than the quantity of girls.There are non-teaching stafs from Apf who helps in many things.Nepal APF School established in the year 2062 BS(AD 2006) is a non profit making organization. It is encircled with greenery occupying 110 ropani of land. It is a school run by Armed Police Force Nepal under the contribution of Armed Police Force Welfare Fund. It is fully an English medium residential school imparting quality education to the wards of APF personnel as well as the civilians.

Admission Process

The admission process is simple and consists of filling out an application, an entrance exam, and an interview with the faculty. Visit the school office for more details.
